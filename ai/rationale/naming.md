# Naming Rationale

Why: `ai/rules/naming.md`

## The Pun
"Ze" = "The" with a French accent. Use "ze" where "the" would work grammatically. "Start ze BGP daemon."

## YANG Separation
- Wire format identifier `"format": "ze-bgp"` is separate from YANG module names
- Socket names and binary names are also separate from YANG names
- Don't change one thinking it affects the other
